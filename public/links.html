neocities vs nekoweb

you can use either one but i've decided to go with neocities because i made my account before 2024 when a lot of limitations were put in place. for example if you made a neocities account now and used the same guestbook as me it would not work. so if you don't own a neocities account i'd recommend nekoweb.

neocities is a lot popular whereas nekoweb seems more like a tightknit community (all of the top sites link to each other)

neocities supports git for free whereas on nekoweb it is a paid feature

nekoweb gives you a little display for your website on the browser for you to customise

crawlers, scrappers, and trackers are blocked on nekoweb whereas neocities doesn't do anything to stop it

ultimately it doesn't matter that much and you could just make a website on both and make one redirect to other (e.g. dimdev), or make them have seperate layouts (however that might be tedious as you would have to update two websites)
